# UW Medicine faculty list (researchers)

`uwmedicine_faculty_all.csv` is populated with **researchers and faculty** (not IT/admin staff).

- **Source:** [BIME – Biomedical Informatics and Medical Education](https://bime.uw.edu/faculty_type/bime-core-extended-faculty/) (UW School of Medicine). BIME focuses on biomedical/health informatics, data science, ML, and clinical informatics, which align with Data Scientist / Data Engineering outreach.
- **Filter:** Only faculty with `@uw.edu` or `@cs.washington.edu` (or `@washington.edu`) are included.
- **Research centers:** The [UW Research Centers (School of Medicine)](https://research.washington.edu/centers/?college=School%20of%20Medicine) page lists centers (e.g. AIMS, Cancer Vaccine Institute). BIME is the main informatics/data-science unit; other center-specific faculty can be added later from their respective center or department pages.
